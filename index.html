About Me

Welcome! I'm Greta Belegu, a dedicated front-end web developer with a passion for creating immersive and user-friendly digital experiences. Equipped with the latest industry knowledge and techniques, I bring a cutting-edge approach to my work.

I graduated in 2022 with a degree in Web Development, with two minors in Computer Science and English. As a first-generation college graduate, I've overcome unique challenges with determination and perseverance. In my portfolio, you'll find a showcase of projects that highlight my expertise in front-end development, responsive design, and creative problem-solving. I'm eager to collaborate, innovate, and contribute to impactful projects. Feel free to chat with me about anything!
